Current Account

A Current Account is a transactional bank account primarily for businesses, allowing unlimited transactions, cheque payments, and overdraft facilities. Current Account Features

Unlimited Transactions

Current Accounts allow unlimited deposits, withdrawals, and transfers, facilitating seamless business transactions without transactional restrictions.

Chequebook Facility

Offers a chequebook for convenient payment transactions, allowing businesses to make payments easily.

Minimum Balance Requirement:

Some accounts mandate a minimum balance, ensuring account stability and eligibility for certain benefits.

Service Charges

May impose fees for specific transactions or account maintenance, varying based on the bank's policies.

Nominal Interest or None

Current Accounts typically don't earn interest or offer minimal interest on balances, focusing on transactional convenience rather than savings.
