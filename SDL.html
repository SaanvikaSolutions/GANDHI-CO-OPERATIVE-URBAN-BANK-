Features and Benefits of Safe Deposit Locker

When you open Ghandi Cooperative Bank NRI FD, rest assured that you will get the following benefits:

  1. Secure Storage: Provides a secure place to store valuable items, documents, and valuables, offering protection against theft, loss, or damage.
  2. Confidentiality: Ensures privacy and confidentiality of stored items, as only the locker holder and authorized personnel have access.
  3. Insurance Coverage:Some banks offer insurance coverage for items stored in safe deposit lockers, providing additional financial protection.
  4. Accessibility Allows convenient access to stored items during banking hours, providing flexibility for retrieval or deposit.
  5. Legal Compliance: Facilitates compliance with legal requirements for safekeeping of important documents and valuables, such as property deeds, wills, and jewelry.
  6. Nomination Facility: Enables the nomination of a beneficiary who can access the locker in case of the holder's demise, ensuring smooth succession.
