About Us

The Gandhi Co-operative Urban Bank Ltd., strictly follow all 7 core principles adopted by the International Co-operative Alliance in 1995 to serving the members. Its primary customer base likely comprises individuals, small businesses, farmers, and others from the local community. The bank was established with the aim of providing financial services and support to the local community. It may have been named after Mahatma Gandhi, symbolizing principles of self-sufficiency and community development.
