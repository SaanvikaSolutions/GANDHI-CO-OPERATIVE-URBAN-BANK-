Eligibility and Documents

Eligibility for Business Loan
  • Viable business model with stable revenue streams.
  • Clear repayment plan.
  • Satisfactory credit history.
  • Minimum turnover requirements.
Documents for Business Loan
  • Business registration documents (Certificate of Incorporation, Partnership Deed, etc.).
  • Business financial statements (Balance Sheet, Profit & Loss Statement, Tax Returns, etc.).
  • Business plan detailing the purpose of the loan and expected fund utilization.
  • Proof of business ownership (Property documents, Lease/Rental Agreement, etc.).
  • Latest passport size 6 Photos.
  • Bank statements reflecting business transactions.
  • KYC documents: (any one from the list mentioned below)
    • PassportPAN Card
    • Driving license
    • Aadhaar Card with date of birth
    • Voters Id

How to apply for a Business Loan ?

  • Visit the Business Loan section.
  • Provide your Business details such as mobile number and either Date of Birth or PANCARD.
  • Key in the OTP that you receive on your mobile number.
  • Give the consent for sharing your details for the purpose of verification of your documents.
  • Keep your last six months bank statements and salary slips ready for uploading.
  • Complete the Video KYC process for your loan application to be complete.
