Eligibility and Documents

Eligibility for House Loan
  • Employment: Salaried individuals, self-employed professionals, and business owners are eligible.
    • Salaried doctors
    • Employees of public and private limited companies
    • Government sector employees including Public Sector Undertaking, Central and Local bodies
  • Age: Minimum age of 21 years and maximum age of 60 years at the time of loan maturity.
  • Income: Minimum net monthly income requirement, with specific thresholds for different loan products.
  • Credit History: A good credit score and repayment history are preferred.
  • Documentation: Applicants need to provide proof of identity, address, income, and other relevant documents as per bank requirements.
  • Loan Purpose: The loan should be utilized for the intended purpose, such as purchasing a house, construction, renovation, or extension.
Documents for Personal Loan

  • House Loan @ 12% (1.00 paisa monthly).
