INTREST RATES

The DICGC insures all types of deposits, including Savings, Fixed, Current, and Recurring deposits. Each depositor in a bank is insured up to a maximum of ₹ 5,00,000 (Rupees Five Lakhs), covering both the principal and interest amounts held in the same right and capacity. Deposits held in different branches of the same bank are aggregated for insurance purposes, with a maximum coverage limit of ₹ 5,00,000. A 4% interest rate is provided for savings accounts.
