Education Loan

Loans Upto Rs. 20,00,000/- under this category are typically sanctioned to fund higher education for applicants' children, covering expenses for postgraduate programs, professional courses, engineering, medicine, and other master's degrees. Applicants must provide cost estimates from the institutions where their children plan to study, including tuition fees, hostel charges, and other necessary supplies.
Interest Rates: 12% for Priority Sector and 12.5% for Non-Priority Sector. Monthly interest payments will be collected from the applicants or guardians until their children secure employment. The loan repayment period will be determined by the Board of Management, based on the course of study undertaken by the applicant's children.

Eligibility and Documents

Eligibility for Educational Loan
  • The applicant must meet the requirements outlined for House Mortgage loans.
  • Applicants must provide estimates from educational institutions for higher studies, including tuition fees, hostel charges, and other essential expenses.
  • Income: Minimum net monthly income requirement, with specific thresholds for different loan products.
  • Credit History: A good credit score and repayment history are preferred.
  • Priority is given to students pursuing higher education in fields such as Post Graduation, Professional Courses, Engineering, Medicine, and other Master's degrees.
  • The applicant's children are the primary beneficiaries of the educational loan.
Documents for Educational Loan
  • Proof of identity (e.g., Aadhar Card, Passport)
  • Proof of residence (e.g., Voter ID, utility bills)
  • Admission letter from the educational institution
  • Estimate of expenses from the institution.
  • Latest passport size 6 Photos.
  • Income proof of the applicant or co-applicant
  • Academic records of the student
  • KYC documents: (any one from the list mentioned below)
    • PassportPAN Card
    • Driving license
    • Aadhaar Card with date of birth
    • Voters Id

How to apply for a Educational Loan ?

  • Interest Rates: 12% for Priority Sector and 12.5% for Non-Priority Sector.
  • Visit the Educational Loan section
  • Provide your Educational details such as mobile number and either Date of Birth or PAN
  • Give the consent for sharing your details for the purpose of verification of your documents
  • Keep your last six months bank statements and salary slips
  • Complete the KYC process for your loan application to be complete
