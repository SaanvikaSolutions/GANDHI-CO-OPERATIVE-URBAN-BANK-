Against Deposits Loan

Gandhi Cooperative Urban Bank Ltd. offers convenient loans against deposits, providing financial support to those in need. With loans not exceeding 85% of the fixed deposit or cash certificate value, clients can access funds swiftly. Interest rates are competitive, just 2% above the deposit rate. Upon deposit maturity, loans are adjusted, with surplus amounts transferred to savings accounts or provided via bankers cheque. Our streamlined process ensures quick access to funds while maximizing returns on deposits.

Interest Rate: The interest on the loan will be 2% above the deposit rate. Upon maturity of the deposit, the loan amount, including principal and interest, will be adjusted against the maturity proceeds. Any surplus will be transferred to the depositor's savings account. If the depositor does not have a savings account with the bank, the surplus proceeds will be held in suspense and paid via a banker's cheque.

Eligibility and Documents

Eligibility for Against Deposits Loan
  • Must be an account holder with Gandhi Cooperative Urban Bank Ltd.
  • Minimum deposit tenure requirements apply.
  • Loan amount not to exceed 85% of the deposit value.
  • Credit History: A good credit score and repayment history are preferred.
Documents for Against Deposits Loan
  • Proof of identity (e.g., Aadhar Card, Passport)
  • Proof of residence (e.g., Voter ID, utility bills)
  • Fixed deposit receipt or cash certificate.
  • Latest passport size 6 Photos.
  • Income proof of the applicant or co-applicant
  • KYC documents: (any one from the list mentioned below)
    • PassportPAN Card
    • Driving license
    • Aadhaar Card with date of birth
    • Voters Id

How to apply for a Against Deposits Loan ?

  • Interest will be charged on the loan @ 2% extra above the Deposit rate.
  • Visit the Against Deposits Loan section.
  • Provide your Against Deposits details such as mobile number and either Date of Birth or PANCARD.
  • Give the consent for sharing your details for the purpose of verification of your documents.
  • Keep your last six months bank statements and salary slips ready.
  • Complete the Video KYC process for your loan application to be complete.
