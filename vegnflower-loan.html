Eligibility and Documents

Eligibility for Vegetables & Flowery Culture Loan
  • Must be an account holder with Gandhi Cooperative Urban Bank Ltd.
  • Citizenship: Indian citizens.
  • Age: Minimum 21 years and maximum 60 years at the time of loan maturity.
  • Employment: Salaried individuals, self-employed professionals, and businessmen.
  • Income: Minimum net monthly income of Rs. 15,000 for existing customers and Rs. 25,000 for new customers.
  • Credit Score: Good credit history and score.
Documents for Vegetables & Flowery Culture Loan
  • Proof of identity (e.g., Aadhar Card, Passport)
  • Proof of residence (e.g., Voter ID, utility bills)
  • Employment Proof: Appointment letter, Employment ID card, or Business registration documents.
  • Latest passport size 6 Photos.
  • Bank Statements: For the last 6 months.
  • Income proof of the applicant or co-applicant
  • KYC documents: (any one from the list mentioned below)
    • PassportPAN Card
    • Driving license
    • Aadhaar Card with date of birth
    • Voters Id

How to apply for a Vegetables & Flowery Culture Loan online?

  • Visit the Vegetables & Flowery Culture Loan section and click on the 'Apply Now' button
  • Provide your Vegetables & Flowery Culture details such as mobile number and either Date of Birth or PAN
